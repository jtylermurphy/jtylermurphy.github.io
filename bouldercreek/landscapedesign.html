LANDSCAPE & DESIGN

A full-service landscape and lawncare company, Boulder Creek offers a wealth of Landscape Maintenance services outside of traditional Landscape & Design.

Sarah and Will aim to build lasting relationships with all landscaping clients. There are many different landscaping products on the market, and we strive to incorporate our customers’ personality and lifestyle needs in every design. We have several different suppliers that we source our materials from, allowing us to create beautiful hard- and soft-scapes for any purpose. Whether you need a small planter bed, a retaining wall, a paver patio, a fire pit, or a pergola, we can make your outdoor living space a unique place for you and your loved ones to relax and unwind.

After hardscapes are installed, it helps to bolster your yard’s appearance with plant material. Whether it’s the color of the blooms, the aroma of the flowers, or the wispiness of grasses blowing in the wind, softscapes with plant material add an appealing natural aesthetic to any patio.

However, there are variables that need to be considered when using plant material as well — sun, shade and moisture are just a few. Let us help you! If you have a new construction project in mind or if you need help with your existing landscaping, no project is too big or too small for our team. Boulder Creek Lawn and Landscape has the ability to handle any project from start to finish. Sarah and Will know that your landscape project is a large investment, and both are always just a phone call away if you have any questions before, during, or after the entire project.
