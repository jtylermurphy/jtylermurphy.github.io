My Work

Just a few of the projects that i've had the pleasure of working on.

KWIK KAR

Kwik Kar needed a new online presence that combined several chain location websites into one main website with a CMS backend that was easy for a non-web developer to use. I incorporated branding elements from their marketing materials to create a site that ties together marketing efforts and branding for these Kwik Kar locations.

Steepl Peepl

My church needed an online directory so members could have access to other member’s contact information on the go. My solution was to develop a responsive Ruby on Rails web application for my church that was easy enough for the secretary to update regularly. Once I completed the app for my church, I decided the next step was to open source the web app so other churches could benefit from it. Steepl Peepl (still in development) is that effort.

Perceptive Experience

At perceptive (Lexmark) I get to work on a team that is revolutionizing the way the company is moving forward with enterprise content managment software. Instead of countless native apps, we pushed for a responsive web experience, and created a platform of UI controls and components that can be used to create targeted persona driven apps instead of boring bloated enterprise software.
